Essentials about
RAIT ACM

RAIT ACM is a Student Chapter of the Elite International level ACM organization. Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) is the world's largest educational and scientific computing society, delivering resources that advance computing as a science and profession.

RAIT ACM SIGAI

RAIT ACM SIGAI stands for Special Interest Group AI. Established this year, this committee focuses on Artificial Intelligence. Artificial Intelligence (AI) is transforming industries and job markets today.

RAIT ACM-W

Supporting and celebrating all women with the motivation and helpful resources, RAIT ACM-W set into action on 9th of February 2023 and is going ahead headstrong.

RAIT ACM Student Chapter

It is the main and founding committee focused on the overall development and progress of students in RAIT. We work in different fields like Technical, Creative, Publicity, Video Editing, Management, Social Media, and Editorial.

Domains of
RAIT ACM

In all fields of technology, we have domains that offer every individual the opportunity to grow.

Technical

The technical team organizes technical events and activities. Assuring the website is updated and providing technical assistance is the team's responsibility.

Management

All the activities held throughout the year are overseen by the management team. Events are tracked and problems encountered during events are handled by this domain.

Editorial

Our editorial team composes the blog and encourages people to read it. The team produces high quality research articles along with publicity messages and event reports.

Publicity

Our publicity team promotes our events and workshops on social media platforms. They inform the audience why they should register and the benefits they will receive.

Creative

The Creative Team makes sure all the creative work is done like making posters, cards, or techfest decorations. They are the backbone of any big event and ensure decorations are put up properly.

Media Graphics

Videos are produced for events by the video editing team. In addition, the team makes sure that the online events are properly recorded and uploaded to the YouTube channel for further reference.

Design

Our design team crafts vibrant banners and posters that capture the essence of each workshop and event. We bring creativity and innovation to every visual detail using computer generated graphics.

Sponsorship

The sponsorship team secures vital partnerships to fuel our hackathons and Techfest. We connect with industry leaders to bring valuable resources and opportunities to all participants.

Technothon 2023

Technothon 2023, hosted by RAIT ACM, was a premier national-level coding competition that brought together some of the brightest minds in data structures and algorithms. This intense battle of logic and problem-solving challenged participants to push their skills to the limit, with a grand prize of ₹20,000 awaiting the ultimate champion. The event showcased an electrifying atmosphere of innovation and competitiveness, culminating in a thrilling victory by Aditya Belgaonkar, who emerged as the winner.
